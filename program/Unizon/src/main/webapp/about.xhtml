FAQ

Do you provide international delivery?

Yes, we do. We can deliver to the following regions: all EU countries, Russia, Switzerland, North America, Mexico, Argentina, Brazil, Chile, Dubai, Japan, South-Korea, Australia and New-Zealand. If you’d like to ship your item to different region, please contact us for help.

How do I track my order?

In the verification e-mail we sent you after your purchase you can find a link and a tracking number below “Track my order” section. Once you click on this link you will see our courier partner’s website where you can type in your tracking number. Once you click on search you will find the latest updates about your shipment.

How do I return an item?

It's so easy, here's all you need to know. Locate the available return method(s) listed on the packing slip. We recently made some changes to our packing slip and return labels. Select your preferred method of return. Remove the original shipping label from the outside of the package and replace it with the selected return label. Get your package to the Post Office or to UPS (based on the selected return method). That’s it!

How can I contact your couries?

Our courier partner company will contact you via email 24 hours before the start of the delivery process. They will give you the estimated arrival and the contact numbers as well. In the meantime, you can contact us for more information and we kindly forward your inquiry to the courier company.

What is your returns policy?

We guarantee our merchandise to be free of manufacturing defects and will accept any defective item for refund or exchange. uniZON will issue a refund within 30 days of purchase in the form and amount tendered for the original transaction. All returns or exchanges must be unused and in their original condition with receipt and tags attached. We will only refund shipping charges if we sent the wrong item or the item sent was defective.

What are your delivery options?

We have a contracted courier partner who can provide fast international delivery. Please be aware that delivery times depend on shipping country and address. Alternatively, you can select Post Office delivery, but it takes 4-6 weeks to ship your item to your location.
